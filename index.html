A daily link worth reading curated by Reuben Ingber

We spend hours in front of screens, but we often leave them empty and unfulfilled, still seeking real knowledge and new information. One Read is meant to change that; it is a simple e-newsletter sent at what may seem like an untraditional time. One Read will hit your inbox between 4 and 5 PM EST each week day with just one link for your afternoon commute. The goal is to teach subscribers something new everyday.
